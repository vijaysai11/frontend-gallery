Your story,
unfolding

01
Day
Delhi → Manali · 530 km
The Overnight Passage

Your adventure begins at the Delhi Volvo pick-up point. As city lights fade, the Himalayas begin their quiet call. Sleep through the night and wake to sparkling mountain air.

Volvo AC overnight coach
02
Day
Manali Local Tour · 3–4 hrs
Temples, Springs & Mall Road

Greeted by our team at Manali stop. Check-in at 12:00 noon, then an afternoon of discovery — Hadimba Mata Temple, Ghatotkacha Temple, Club House, Tibetan Monastery, healing Vashisht Hot Springs, and the lively Mall Road.

Hotel · Dinner · Car
03
Day
Solang Valley · 15 km from Manali
The Valley of Adventure

Head to Solang Valley — nature's ultimate adventure playground. Paragliding, zorbing, ATV rides, horse riding, and trail treks against a backdrop of snow-capped peaks. Optional: Atal Tunnel drive (extra cost).

Hotel · Breakfast · Dinner · Car
04
Day
Kullu · Manikaran · 80 km
Rivers, Rapids & Sacred Springs

Drive through Kullu for white-water river rafting on the Beas, halt at Devlok Manali and Pashmina showrooms, then journey to Manikaran — sacred hot springs, the revered Gurudwara, and temples of Rama, Krishna and Vishnu. The Langar nourishes body and soul.

Hotel · Breakfast · Dinner · Car
05
Day
Manali → Delhi · Departure
The Journey Home

A final morning on Mall Road — souvenirs, Van Vihar Park strolls, and quiet reflection. Store luggage at BBH Cloakroom. Your Volvo departs; the mountains bid farewell until next time.

Breakfast · Volvo AC coach
01
Day
Srinagar Airport Arrival
Welcome to Paradise

Our representative greets you at Srinagar Airport and transfers you to your hotel. After freshening up, explore the famous Mughal Gardens — Nishat Bagh (Garden of Pleasure), Shalimar Bagh (Abode of Love), and Chashme Shahi with its natural spring water. Dinner and overnight stay in Srinagar.

Hotel · Dinner · Airport Transfer
02
Day
Gulmarg · Full Day Excursion
Meadows of Gold & Gondola Skies

A full-day in Gulmarg — lush green meadows framed by snow-capped peaks. Ride the famous Gulmarg Gondola (own cost), explore on horseback, or embrace seasonal snow adventures. Return to Srinagar by evening.

Hotel · Breakfast · Dinner · Cab
03
Day
Sonamarg · Meadow of Gold
Glaciers & the Sindh River

Drive to Sonamarg and witness breathtaking Himalayan landscapes, glaciers, and the roaring Sindh River. Optional pony ride to Thajiwas Glacier (own cost) and Zero Point sightseeing. Return to Srinagar by evening.

Hotel · Breakfast · Dinner · Cab
04
Day
Pahalgam · Valley of Shepherds
Saffron Fields & Sacred Valleys

Drive to Pahalgam. En route, visit saffron fields and the historic Awantipora Ruins. Explore Lidder River and the scenic valleys — Aru Valley, Betaab Valley, and Chandanwari (local union taxi, own cost). Overnight in Pahalgam.

Hotel · Breakfast · Dinner · Cab
05
Day
Pahalgam → Srinagar · Departure
Until Kashmir Calls Again

After breakfast, drive back to Srinagar and transfer to the airport for your onward journey. Carry with you beautiful memories of the Valley of Paradise.

Breakfast · Airport Drop · Cab
01
Day
Delhi → Manali · 530 km
The Overnight Passage

Board your Volvo in Delhi as the city fades into the night. The highway winds north through the plains and into the mountains. Wake to crisp Himalayan air in Manali.

Volvo AC overnight coach
02
Day
Manali City Tour
Temples, Springs & Mall Road

Check in at noon, then afternoon sightseeing: Hadimba Mata Temple, Ghatotkacha Temple, Club House, Tibetan Monastery, healing Vashisht Hot Springs, and the vibrant Mall Road in the evening.

Hotel · Dinner · Car
03
Day
Solang Valley / Atal Tunnel
Adventure Above the Clouds

Solang Valley for paragliding, zorbing, ATV rides and horse riding. Or opt for a drive through Atal Tunnel — one of the world's highest tunnels — into the Lahaul & Spiti Valley (extra cost, inform at booking).

Hotel · Breakfast · Dinner · Car
04
Day
Kullu → Naggar · 20–40 km
Rafting, Castles & Russian Art

Drive to Kullu for white-water river rafting on the Beas. Stop at Devlok Manali and Pashmina showrooms, then head to Naggar Castle — former seat of Kullu kings, now a heritage landmark. Visit the Roerich Art Gallery and glimpse Tripura Sundri Temple (one of India's 51 Shakti Peethas) enroute. Freshen up at a Trout Fish Farm on the way back. Note: Roerich Gallery closed Mondays.

Hotel · Breakfast · Dinner · Car
05
Day
Manali → Shimla · 240 km · 7–8 hrs
Heading to the Queen of Hills

After breakfast, check out from Manali and board a sharing deluxe bus or cab to Shimla. En route, stop at Mata Vaishno Devi Temple in Kullu and admire the grand Pandoh Dam. Check in at Shimla hotel for dinner and rest.

Hotel · Breakfast · Dinner · Sharing Bus/Cab
06
Day
Kufri + Snow Kingdom + Shimla Heritage
Thrills & Colonial Grandeur

Morning at Kufri and Snow Kingdom — horse riding, yak riding, zip-lining, tobogganing and cycling. Afternoon in Shimla's iconic heart: Scandal Point, The Ridge, Gaiety Theatre, Heritage Museum, Lakkar Bazaar, Rani Jhansi Park and Christ Church.

Hotel · Breakfast · Dinner · Car
07
Day
Shimla → Delhi · Daytime Journey
The Journey Home

After breakfast, check out and head to the Volvo stand for your daytime journey back to Delhi. Seven days of mountains, memories and magic — safely, beautifully delivered.

Breakfast · Volvo AC coach
